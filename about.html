NAMM Show

(National Association of Music Merchants)
@East Africa

The NAMM show is an annual event that is organized by the National Association of Music Merchants (NAMM), who describe it as "the industry's largest stage, uniting the global music, sound and entertainment technology communities". It is one of the largest music product trade shows in the world.

The event attracts numerous Legendary and famous musicians, many of whom are endorsed by exhibitors and come to promote their own signature models and equipment.

Please contact on our website form for any further questions about The NAMM Show East Africa 2023!

The Namm Show 2023 Logo

The logo of The Namm Show 2023 was decided through the logo competition from 8th February to 7th March.

"Jim Cruickshank" won the competition. He had an eye for design! While a proud member of the Fender guitar team, Jim designed many of the most memorable trade show displays for the NAMM company.
